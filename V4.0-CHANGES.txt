BLOCK WORLD PERU – V4.0

- Replaced the lower-right collection control with a hand-drawn pixel backpack PNG.
- Added 24 independent transparent PNG collectible assets; no sprite sheet and no cutting.
- Backpack now displays the complete collection catalogue from the beginning.
- Locked items appear as dark grey silhouettes with hidden names.
- Collected items light up in full colour and show duplicate counts.
- Treasure chests now award 100 coins plus one random ancient object, gemstone or toy.
- Plant harvests now award 100 coins plus a corresponding fruit or seed.
- Collection progress is saved in localStorage and displayed in the backpack.
